Week 3 - Day 13

Final Project Preparation

Methodology

Outline the step-by-step approach for developing your final project:

  • Define problem statement and objectives
  • Identify required hardware components and software tools
  • Design the system architecture and data flow
  • Develop and test each module independently
  • Integrate modules and validate the full system
  • Visualize data and generate insights

Study of Existing Projects

Research and analyze similar existing IoT and ML-based projects to gain ideas and understand challenges:

  • Identify key features and limitations of those projects
  • Analyze technologies used and their integration
  • Evaluate their user interface and data presentation

Reviewing Research Papers

Read and review scholarly articles related to your project domain:

  • Identify current trends and gaps in IoT or ML applications
  • Understand theoretical frameworks and evaluation metrics
  • Incorporate relevant concepts into your methodology

Reading Review Papers

Explore review papers to get a broader understanding of the research landscape and future opportunities:

  • Summarize key technologies and comparison between them
  • Note significant advancements and possible improvements
  • Use insights to refine your project design
